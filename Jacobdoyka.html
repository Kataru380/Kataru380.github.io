<h1><b><i>New Coffee Shop Around Town</i></b></h1>

<h2>By: Jacob Doyka</h2>

<p>Something that is new and happening in Windber is a new coffee shop. The coffee shop is called The Rustic Blend Cafe. They have a website with online ordering and a phone number to call in. It is located on Graham avenue. They offer a lot of different drinks and food.</p>

<p>THey offer a wide selection of food and drink. They offer bagels, croissants, yogurt, muffins, bacon sticks and much more for breakfast. All of their food is under $6.00. The drinks that they have are a wide selection of coffee and tea. They have a lot of flavored syrups and sometimes they have special drinks for the day. The reviews have been positive and most are five stars.

<blockquote>”The coffee is amazing, fast and cheap. The atmosphere feels homey and the staff is excellent. I will definitely come back here.”</blockquote></p>

<p>The Rustic Blend also does things for the local community. They raise money for charities and even the local humane society. Some nights they will have painting classes or study time for students. The atmosphere is very comfy and cozy. All are welcomed and the staff is friendly.</p>
